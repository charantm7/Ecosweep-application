    Overview

    Karnataka, a state in southwest India, is known for its diverse culture, history, and natural beauty. With Bengaluru as its capital, it's a hub for technology and innovation, often called the "Silicon Valley of India." Karnataka's history includes the Vijayanagara Empire, whose ruins in Hampi are a UNESCO World Heritage site. The state has a rich blend of natural attractions, from the Western Ghats' lush forests and wildlife to coastal beaches like Gokarna and historic sites like Mysuru Palace. Karnataka is also famous for its distinct cuisine, known for dishes like bisi bele bath, ragi mudde, and Mysore pak.
